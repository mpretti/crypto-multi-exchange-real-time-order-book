## 🤖 **AI Agents Guide**

### **1. Simple Moving Average (Beginner)**
- **Strategy**: Golden Cross - Buy when fast MA > slow MA
- **Best For**: Learning trend following concepts
- **Typical Performance**: 12-18% annual return, 65% win rate
- **Risk Level**: Low to Medium

### **2. RSI Mean Reversion (Intermediate)**
- **Strategy**: Buy oversold (RSI < 30), sell overbought (RSI > 70)
- **Best For**: Range-bound markets and counter-trend trading
- **Typical Performance**: 15-22% annual return, 58% win rate
- **Risk Level**: Medium

### **3. Deep Q-Learning (Advanced)**
- **Strategy**: Neural network with experience replay and pattern recognition
- **Best For**: Complex market conditions and adaptive strategies
- **Typical Performance**: 20-35% annual return, 72% win rate
- **Risk Level**: Medium to High

### **4. Custom Strategy (Expert)**
- **Strategy**: User-defined rules using the Visual Strategy Builder
- **Best For**: Testing personal ideas and advanced algorithms
- **Performance**: Varies based on strategy design
- **Risk Level**: Configurable

## 📊 **Key Metrics Explained**

### **Performance Metrics**
- **Total Return**: Portfolio growth percentage
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted returns (>1.0 is good, >2.0 is excellent)
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of profitable trades
- **Profit Factor**: Gross profit divided by gross loss

### **Risk Metrics**
- **Value at Risk (VaR)**: Potential loss at 95% confidence
- **Expected Shortfall**: Average loss when VaR is exceeded
- **Beta**: Correlation with market movements
- **Alpha**: Excess returns vs. benchmark
